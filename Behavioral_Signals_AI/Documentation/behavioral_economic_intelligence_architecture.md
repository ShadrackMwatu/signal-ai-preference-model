# Behavioral Signals AI: Revealed Preference and Aggregate Demand Intelligence Architecture

Behavioral Signals AI is now organized as an AI-native behavioral-economic intelligence system. Its purpose is to infer revealed behavior, revealed preferences, aggregate demand, economic pressure, emerging market opportunities, and response strategies from aggregate digital and social behavioral signals.

This is not an individual surveillance system. Signal uses aggregate public indicators only and does not infer personal behavior, identify people, or target individuals.

## Core Theory

The system is grounded in:

- revealed preference theory;
- behavioral economics;
- aggregate demand inference;
- digital behavioral traces;
- market intelligence;
- AI-assisted economic interpretation.

Modern revealed-preference signals include clicks, searches, likes, comments, shares, saves, repeat engagement, ignored products, delayed actions, complaints, comparison behavior, and public concern. Signal treats these as aggregate behavioral demand signals when they are available in privacy-preserving form.

## Backend Layers

```text
Behavioral_Signals_AI/
  backend/                       pipeline orchestration and ingestion
  analytics/                     normalized aggregate signal metrics
  revealed_preference_engine/    revealed interest, need, pressure, demand
  behavioral_inference/          aggregate behavioral-economic interpretation
  demand_intelligence/           demand classification and unmet demand
  opportunity_engine/            market gap and opportunity inference
  recommendation_engine/         business and policy recommendations
  forecasting/                   persistence, volatility, trend continuation
  market_intelligence/           market summaries and action framing
```

## Runtime Flow

```text
aggregate public trend records
  -> Behavioral Signal Ingestion Engine
  -> Analytics Signal Metrics
  -> Revealed Preference Engine
  -> Behavioral Inference Engine
  -> Demand Intelligence Engine
  -> Opportunity Intelligence Engine
  -> Forecasting Engine
  -> Recommendation Engine
  -> Market Intelligence Summary
  -> Dashboard: what is trending, what it reveals, where, opportunity, action
```

## Dashboard Philosophy

The public dashboard should answer five questions:

1. What is trending?
2. What signal does it reveal?
3. Where is it happening?
4. What opportunity does it create?
5. What should I do?

Technical controls, raw model scores, gauges, volatility mechanics, provider internals, and diagnostic-style outputs belong in **Advanced Analytics**, collapsed by default.

## Kenya Phase

The current phase focuses on Kenya-wide intelligence. County-level inference is intentionally limited and will be developed later as a separate layer with stronger geographic evidence.

## Privacy and Ethics

Signal must preserve:

- aggregate intelligence only;
- no personal surveillance;
- no invasive profiling;
- no individual-level targeting;
- no unauthorized scraping;
- no storage of private personal data.

## Production Transition

This architecture prepares Behavioral Signals AI for future marketplace, logistics, supplier matching, payment, API, adaptive-learning, forecasting, and county-intelligence integrations while keeping the current Hugging Face deployment lightweight.
