# Market Clearing

Market-clearing equations require supply to equal demand in commodity and factor markets.

Placeholder structure:

```text
commodity_supply(c) = intermediate_demand(c) + household_demand(c) + government_demand(c) + investment_demand(c) + exports(c)
factor_supply(f) = sum_a factor_demand(f,a)
```

Future solver diagnostics should report residuals and identify the redundant market condition implied by Walras' law.
