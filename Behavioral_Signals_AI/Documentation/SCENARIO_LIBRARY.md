# Scenario Library

Signal includes baseline, tax policy, care economy, infrastructure, and trade policy templates. Care-economy scenarios include double paid care services, converting unpaid care to paid care, care infrastructure investment, social-services investment, gender-disaggregated labour income effects, and household welfare effects using suffixes `fcp`, `fcu`, `fnp`, `fnu`, `mcp`, `mcu`, `mnp`, and `mnu`.
