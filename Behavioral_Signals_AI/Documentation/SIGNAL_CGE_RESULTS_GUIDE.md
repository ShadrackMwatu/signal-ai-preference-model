# Signal CGE Results Guide

## Level Results

Level results report model values after simulation, such as output, imports, exports, household income, government revenue, and investment.

## Difference From Baseline

Differences compare the policy simulation with the benchmark or baseline value.

## Percentage Change From Baseline

Percentage changes report relative movement from baseline and help compare effects across accounts.

## Macro Results

Macro results include output, absorption, investment, savings, government balance, trade balance, and exchange-rate or external-balance indicators.

## Welfare/Proxy Welfare

Welfare proxies use household real income or consumption-related indicators until a full welfare module is active.

## Structural Change

Structural change reports shifts across activities, commodities, factors, trade, and institutions.

## Trade Effects

Trade effects include import prices, import demand, exports, tariff revenue, and trade balance.

## Household Effects

Household effects report income, expenditure, real-income proxy, and distributional indicators where household accounts are available.

## Factor Income Effects

Factor income effects report labor, capital, land, or other factor-income changes where factor accounts are available.

## Government Balance Effects

Government balance effects report revenue, spending, savings, deficit pressure, and replacement-tax implications where closure rules define the adjustment.
